Food Outlets

Guests can enjoy a variety of food outlets on-site or bring their own picnic for a personalized dining experience.


-Here are the some on-site outlets at ZooParc-



Themed Cafes and Restaurant

Our themed cafes and restaurant offer a unique dining experience, immersing you in the magic of the animal kingdom. Each establishment is designed with a distinct theme, inspired by different regions and habitats.


Special Features:
- Themed decor and ambiance
- Menu options for all dietary preferences
- Interactive dining experiences with animal presentations



Food Trucks and Stalls

Our food trucks and stalls offer a variety of quick, delicious, and convenient options for visitors on the go. From gourmet snacks to hearty meals, there's something for everyone.


Special Features:
- Rotating food truck lineup with new offerings regularly
- Conveniently located near popular exhibits
- Quick service for a fast yet satisfying meal



Healthy and Sustainable Choices

Committed to promoting a healthy lifestyle and sustainability, our outlets offer a range of nutritious and eco-friendly food options. Enjoy delicious meals made with organic, locally-sourced ingredients.


Special Features:
- Eco-friendly packaging and utensils
- Nutritional information provided for all dishes
- Partnerships with local farms and suppliers



Kid-Friendly Options

Our kid-friendly outlets are designed with young visitors in mind, offering fun and nutritious meals that children will love. Each menu is crafted to cater to picky eaters while ensuring balanced nutrition.


Special Features:
- Interactive meal presentations
- Healthy options disguised as fun treats
- Safe and family-friendly dining environments
