About ZooParc

Zooparc is a sanctuary for exotic and endangered species, offering an immersive experience for visitors of all ages. Designed to mimic their natural environments, the team of conservationists and educators promotes biodiversity and conservation efforts, fostering a deeper appreciation for nature. Discover More..

Become a Member

Join the Zooparc community and experience wildlife like never before! As a member, you'll gain exclusive access to our zoological park, where you can explore diverse animal habitats, attend special events, and enjoy behind-the-scenes tours. Volunteering at Zooparc also offers you membership perks after registration, giving you a deeper connection to our conservation efforts and the animals we care for. Become a part of our vibrant community today and make a meaningful impact while enjoying all the benefits of being a Zooparc member!
