About ZooParc


ZooParc Zoological Park is a premier destination for wildlife enthusiasts and families alike. Spanning 70 hectares of beautifully maintained habitats, our zoo is home to about 2,000 animals representing 200 different species from around the globe. Our commitment to conservation, education, and animal welfare makes us a leader in the zoological community.



Our Mission


At ZooParc Zoological Park, our mission is to foster a deep connection between people and wildlife.
We are dedicated to:


  • Conservation: Protecting endangered species and their habitats through active breeding programs and partnerships with global conservation organizations.
  • Education: Providing educational programs for schools and the public to promote awareness and understanding of wildlife and environmental issues.
  • Animal Welfare: Ensuring the highest standards of care for our animals through state-of-the-art facilities and expert veterinary care.


Visitor Experience


  • Interactive Exhibits: Engage with animals up close and personal through our interactive exhibits and feeding sessions.
  • Guided Tours: Learn from our knowledgeable staff during guided tours that offer insights into the lives of our residents.
  • Family Activities: Enjoy a range of activities designed for families, including children's playgrounds, petting zoos, and educational workshops.

Visit Us


Plan your visit to ZooParc Zoological Park today and embark on an unforgettable adventure. Whether you are a local resident or a tourist, our park offers an enriching experience for all ages. We look forward to welcoming you!
